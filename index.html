Welcome to the Storm

We are a middle school and high school summer lacrosse team and our goal is to prepare student athletes for the next level.

The coaches are all individuals who have had years of experience coaching lacrosse from the middle school level to the high school level. Click the button below to find out more information.


Practice Time

Club Team

When it comes to playing for a team during school, most schools in the N. Kentucky area have a team but, if your school does not then a local club team, like the Raiders Lacrosse organization, will be glad to take you on!
